Marks is a private bookmark tracker that keeps your saved articles, tweets, PDFs, and links organized and readable.

SAVE FROM ANYWHERE
Use the share extension to save any link from Safari, Chrome, or any app. Marks automatically detects the content type — articles, tweets, videos, PDFs, and more.

READ LATER, DISTRACTION-FREE
Open saved articles in a clean reader view with no ads or clutter. PDFs render inline. Tweets show their full text and images.

ORGANIZE WITH TAGS
Tag your bookmarks to find them later. Filter by tag, search across titles and URLs, and keep your collection tidy.

SYNC ACROSS DEVICES
Your bookmarks sync securely via Supabase. Access your full collection from your iPhone and the web.

OFFLINE ACCESS
Saved content is cached locally so you can read even without a connection.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your data stays yours. No tracking, no ads, no social features. Just your bookmarks.
